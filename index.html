About Tech For Impact


We empower communities through technology, innovation, and impact-driven solutions.


Tech For Impact is a youth-led initiative leveraging digital tools, education, and local knowledge to transform lives and solve real-world challenges. We believe in the power of technology to uplift communities, improve livelihoods, and inspire sustainable development.
